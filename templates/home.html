What Problem Does HealthBiotic Address?


Skyrocketing costs, unbearable wait times, inefficiencies in drug development in present healthcare systems. We Don't have to solve the same problem twice. HealthBiotic can learn from each past experience and find new patterns and minimize the error.
It isn't about replacing the healthcare jobs but rather helping in fast tracking the process.
Integrating AI Into Current Healthcare Business Flow
Most AI tools are standalone applications requiring analysts to learn the application ,expecting a huge learning curve. HealthBotic like applcations can provide a platform to those solutions.

Integrating AI Expertise Within the Organization
Effective AI integration requires data science expertise.Teaching data science concepts and skills to existing teams is a slow, expensive, and unsustainable process.

Integrating Machine Learning and Biology
HealthBotic platform believe's that explicitly trained models will outperform intuition,tackling challenges across R&D where machine learning and modern biological tools can be combined to train truly enabling predictive models.

Drug Discovery and Development
Machine learning might not be solution to all problems in R&D, but it’s time to bring new and powerful tools to the process which we discover and develop new drugs. Over time, we can become the truly data-driven, integrated, drug discovery and development.
